Department Quality Assurance Cell (DQAC)

The unofficial Department Quality Assurance Cell (DQAC) is designed for the School of Computer Applications and Technology, specifically managed by Dr. Kavita and the Department of Computer Application. This portal facilitates the collection and management of various data, including new admissions, existing student information, faculty details, and research and publication records. The system aims to streamline data handling and support the department in maintaining high-quality standards and effective management practices.
